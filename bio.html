I am Reza and I am from Ghazni, Afghanistan. Afghanistan is located in Southern Asia borders with China, Iran, Tajikistan, Turkmenistan, Pakistan, and Uzbekistan. The geography of Afghanistan is arid and mountainous; the Hindu Kush Mountains run northeast to southwest and divide the northern provinces from the rest of the country. Ghazni is a city found in Ghazni, Afghanistan. It is located 33.55 latitude and 68.42 longitude and it is situated at elevation 2179 meters above sea level. Ghazni has a population of 141,000 making it the biggest city in Ghazni. It operates on the AFT time zone.
